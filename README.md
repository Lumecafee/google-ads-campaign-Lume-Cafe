# 🌿 Google Ads Campaign – LUME Café

## ✨ Overview
This project simulates a Google Ads campaign for *LUME Café*, a premium, design-led coffee brand focused on creating calm, aesthetic, and immersive experiences.

The campaign is built using a combination of **digital marketing strategy and consumer psychology**, emphasizing perception, emotional resonance, and quiet luxury branding.

---

## 🎯 Campaign Objectives
- Increase website traffic  
- Build brand awareness among urban audiences  
- Encourage store visits through high-intent search visibility  

---

## 🧠 Strategy Approach

### 🔍 Search Ads
Targeted high-intent users searching for:
- “best cafes near me”  
- “aesthetic cafes in [city]”  
- “quiet coffee places”  

👉 Focus: capturing *active demand*

---

### 🎨 Display Ads
Visually-driven ads designed with:
- Minimal, neutral color palettes  
- Coffee, nature, and soft lighting aesthetics  
- Emotion-led messaging  

👉 Focus: building *brand perception and recall*

---

### 🧩 Positioning
Unlike traditional café marketing, this campaign avoids:
- Discount-heavy messaging  
- Loud, high-frequency advertising  

Instead, it emphasizes:
> *calm, intentional, and experience-led branding*

---

## 👥 Target Audience

**Demographics:**
- Age: 20–40  
- Location: Urban metro cities (India)  
- Income: Middle to upper-middle class  

**Psychographics:**
- Values aesthetics and slow living  
- Interested in coffee culture, design, and minimal spaces  
- Seeks calm, less crowded environments  

---

## 💰 Budget & Bidding
- Daily Budget: ₹1,000  
- Campaign Duration: 7 days  
- Total Budget: ₹7,000  

**Bidding Strategy:**
- Initial: Maximize Clicks  
- Optimization Phase: Maximize Conversions  

---

## 🔑 Keyword Strategy

**Core Keywords:**
- best cafes near me  
- specialty coffee shop  
- aesthetic cafes  

**Long-tail Keywords:**
- minimal aesthetic cafes Mumbai  
- peaceful coffee places near me  

**Negative Keywords:**
- cheap  
- fast food  
- jobs  

👉 Ensures premium brand positioning is maintained

## 📊 Key Learnings

- Marketing is not just visibility — it is **perception design**  
- Emotional and aesthetic positioning significantly impact engagement  
- Premium brands benefit from **restraint over volume**  
- Long-tail keywords improve targeting efficiency  

---

## ⚠️ Challenges Faced

- High competition for generic keywords  
- Balancing minimal design with performance-driven messaging  
- Maintaining premium positioning while optimizing for clicks  

---

## 💡 Recommendations

- Implement retargeting campaigns  
- A/B test emotional vs functional messaging  
- Improve landing page visual storytelling  
- Increase budget allocation for high-performing keywords
